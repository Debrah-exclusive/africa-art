Analysis Tips

Look Closely

Spend time observing details. African art often contains layers of meaning in small elements.

Consider Function

Many African artworks serve practical, spiritual, or social functions beyond decoration.

Think Symbolically

Colors, patterns, and forms often carry symbolic meanings related to beliefs and values.

Appreciate Rhythm

African aesthetics often emphasize rhythm and repetition rather than realistic representation.
