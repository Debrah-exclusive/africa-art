          Visual Analysis Framework

          Use these questions to analyze African artworks:

          Visual Elements

          • What materials were used?
          • What are the dominant colors and patterns?
          • How is space and form organized?
          • What textures and surfaces are present?

          Cultural Context

          • What culture created this artwork?
          • What was its original function?
          • What spiritual or social meanings does it carry?
          • How does it reflect cultural values?

          Aesthetic Evaluation

          • How does it demonstrate functionality?
          • What symbolic meanings are present?
          • How does it create visual pleasure?
          • Does it maintain cultural authenticity?
